Project Name : Mini-Sentinel

Purpose of the project:

With a car,a Raspberry Pi and this website , the user can control the direction of the car , light on and off 2 LEDs ( act as headlights )
and while filming with a camera attached to it, all this with order to scout over a certain area and moreover we attached a sensor that
warns the user if it is too close to some obstacle.

Instructions for the controls:

|1|. By going to the "Control" page , you will be welcomed with the interface for controlling the car,camera,lights and visualise the data gathered by the sensors. |2|. Press the to move the car forward (W/w key). |3|. Press the to move the car backward (S/s key). |4|. Pres the to steer left (A/a key). |5|. Pres the to steer right (D/d key). |6|. Press the to activate camera. |7|. Press the to light on/off the lights (F/f key).
